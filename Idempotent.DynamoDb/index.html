Idempotent.DynamoDb provides an idempotent store implementation backed by Amazon DynamoDB. It is designed for cloud-native, horizontally scalable systems where low-latency access and managed durability are required.

This store prioritizes scalability and availability. For strict relational guarantees or cross-transaction querying, a SQL-based store may be more appropriate.
